An Untraditional Path

From Hawai'i to Seattle to wherever the wind blows next

With a double major in Marketing and Management and a minor in Innovation and Entrepreneurship, I was ready to dive into the world of marketing with the goal of meeting people, running accounts and strategizing success. Being exposed to website development in current role, however, led to an endless rabbit hole full of challenges that began to excite and inspire me. While I had never seen myself as a front-end developer, I’m glad I took a leap of faith with Shecodes.io and began learning because it’s a career path that has everything I ever imagined and more: logical thinking, creative problem-solving and a constantly changing environment. So bring your marketing needs and let's see what I can create for you!
